The novel contributions of this work are:

  • (a) the first open-source academic iris matchers compliant with the Iris Exchange (IREX) and evaluated in the IREX X program for accuracy, search time, template size, and selected demographics effects),
  • (b) an iris image segmentation approach, incorporating two models: (i) a pixel-wise segmentation model based on a nested U-Net architecture with dilated convolutions, and (ii) a regression model approximating inner and outer iris boundaries directly from the iris image.
  • (c) two neural networks trained using: (i) Triplet loss with Batch-Hard Triplet mining, and (ii) ArcFace loss.
